KApplications builds personal productivity applications based on the idea that you can meet goals and improve your life by modifying your behavior. Whether you want to eat healthy, exercise more, quit smoking, lose weight, save money, or finish a project you have started, KApplications has an app that can help.

Change is a challenge, but one that you CAN conquer. Our apps will help you make better decisions so you can do the things that are important to you. You truly have the power to change your life...and it all starts with changing your habits.

Download Habitude

Do you want to develop good habits? Are you frustrated by failed resolutions? Do you wish you had the willpower to floss everyday, and not just the week before your dentist appointment? Simple habits can be developed one at a time, one day at a time. Habitude is a progress monitoring application to help you build good habits and eliminate bad ones. Viewing a record of past successes can boost your willpower when you feel weak, and help you gear up for future challenges.
