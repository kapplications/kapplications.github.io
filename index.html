ChangeYour Habits... Change your Life

KApplications offers personal productivity applications to help you succeed. Our applications are based on the idea that you can meet your goals and improve your life by modifying your behavior. Whether you want to eat healthy, exercise more, quit smoking, lose weight, save money, or finish a project you have started, KApplications has an app that can help you meet your goal.

Change is a challenge, but one that you CAN conquer. Our apps will help you make better decisions so you can do the things that are important to you. You truly have the power to change your life...and it all starts with changing your habits.

Rather Drive Stick?

If you prefer to not use the automatic generator, push a branch named gh-pages to your repository to create a page manually. In addition to supporting regular HTML content, GitHub Pages support Jekyll, a simple, blog aware static site generator written by our own Tom Preston-Werner. Jekyll makes it easy to create site-wide headers and footers without having to copy them across every page. It also offers intelligent blog support and other advanced templating features.
