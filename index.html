Trash Your Troubles

Free yourself from negative thoughts by taking out your mental garbage.

Download

Are negative thoughts affecting your daily activities, emotions, and relationships? A series of experiments in the journal Psychological Science1 shows that we can free ourselves from the impact of negative thoughts, simply by throwing them away. In the studies, subjects wrote positive or negative thoughts on a piece of paper or into a text document on a computer. They then either saved their thoughts or discarded them away. Later, subjects who had thrown their thoughts away were less affected by them than subjects who had held onto their thoughts.

Throw out your mental garbage. Don’t allow yourself to be held hostage by negative thoughts any longer – Trash Your Troubles today.

1Pablo Briñol, Margarita Gascó, Richard E. Petty, and Javier Horcajo, Treating Thoughts as Material Objects Can Increase or Decrease Their Impact on Evaluation, Psychological Science November 26, 2012, doi: 10.1177/0956797612449176

Don't Eat That!

The art of structured splurging.

Download

The only way to get rid of a temptation is to yield to it. -- Oscar Wilde

Are you constantly struggling with your diet because there are just too many good foods out there to eat? Do you want to make better food choices? The key is to eat healthy most of the time and allow yourself occasional guilt-free cheating. When your colleague brings in donuts on Tuesday morning it can be difficult to turn them down. Don't Eat That! is an application that can help you decide when to say "No" to those donuts or cupcakes, and when to indulge.
